VBS 2021 - God's Wonder Lab

We are forging ahead with our plans to hold a Virtual VBS. The theme verse, Psalm 98:1, “He has done marvelous things!”, shares a word of hope and trust - Jesus does the impossible for us!


Virtual VBS

Virtual VBS takes place via Zoom on July 20, 21, 22 from 9am-11:15am for children from 3 years old to those currently in Grade 5. A fee of $10/child or $15/family will offset some of the costs, with the church covering the rest.


Preparations and Provisions

A Top-notch team of leaders are prepping - Tom our Storyteller, Kierstin our Musician, Pastor our Spiritual Guide, Shelley our Craftswoman, Jane and Sandy our scientists, Tina our games guru, and more.


We will provide the following:

  • Zoom link for each day’s message, games and crafts, etc.
  • Craft Kit with material for each day (to be distributed prior to July 20)
  • Lesson leaflet for the younger students (to be distributed with craft kit)
  • Prayer covering our leaders, families and students to know that Jesus does the impossible for us.


Registration

Anyone using online banking can send the 2021 VBS fees via e-transfer. Prince of Peace is set up to receive e-transfers automatically (no code necessary). In the Memorandum field put all children's names with the family last name so we can match registrations and fee payments.
