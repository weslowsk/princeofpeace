VBS

Vacation Bible School (VBS) 2023 Theme: God's Living Water - Covered in Jesus' Grace


We are well into plans to welcome children to learn about God's Living Water.


Many fun activities, together with friends, include bible stories, games, crafts, snacks, science, bible memory, and songs. Join us for this fantastic opportunity to focus on Jesus, the Living Water. This series stretches from the Israelites to the Early Church, as believers experience the grace of God.


VBS will be held Monday to Friday mornings (9am to noon) from July 10th - July 14th for ages 4 through 12.
