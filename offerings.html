Offering Options


Post dated Cheques

Post dated cheques can be placed inside your offering envelopes and then dropped off at the church office or mailed to:

Prince of Peace Lutheran Church
935 McCarthy Blvd. N.
Regina, SK
S4X 3L2

Pre-Authorized Offerings

Prince of Peace Lutheran Church accepts donations through Pre-Authorized Payment. If you wish to make donations through Pre-Authorized Payment please complete the Pre-Authorized Payment Form and return it to the church or email it to etransfer@princeofpeaceregina.ca.

Interac e-Transfer

Anyone using online banking on a computer or mobile device can send their offerings to Prince of Peace using Interac e-Transfer. Prince of Peace is setup to receive e-transfers automatically. Interac e-Transfers can be sent to etransfer@princeofpeaceregina.ca.
