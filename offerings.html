Offering Options

The Covid-19 pandemic has affected all of us in one way or another, and Prince of Peace Lutheran Church is certainly not immune to these difficult times. To help maintain the operations of the church there are several options available for your generous offerings.


Post dated Cheques

Post dated cheques can be placed inside your offering envelopes and then dropped off at the church office or mailed to Prince of Peace Lutheran Church, 935 McCarthy Blvd. N., Regina, SK S4X 3L2. If you drop off your post-dated cheques at the church, please maintain social distancing by:

Pre-Authorized Offerings

Prince of Peace Lutheran Church accepts donations through Pre-Authorized Payment. If you wish to make donations through Pre-Authorized Payment please complete the Pre-Authorized Payment Form and return it to the church or email it to etransfer@princeofpeaceregina.ca.

Interac e-Transfer

Anyone using online banking on a computer or mobile device can send their offerings to Prince of Peace using Interac e-Transfer. Prince of Peace is setup to receive e-transfers automatically. Interac e-Transfers can be sent to etransfer@princeofpeaceregina.ca.
