Offering Options

The Covid-19 pandemic has affected all of us in one way or another, and Prince of Peace Lutheran Church is certainly not immune to these difficult times. While the giving of an offering can easily be seen merely as necessary to “pay the bills”, as Christian stewards (managers) we view our offering as an act of worship and a means by which we express our gratitude to God for what he has given us, especially in His Son, returning to Him a portion of what is really already His anyway, but that He has entrusted to us. To help maintain the operations of the Church there are several options available for your generous offerings.


Post dated Cheques

Post dated cheques can be placed inside your offering envelopes and then dropped off at the church office or mailed to:

Prince of Peace Lutheran Church
935 McCarthy Blvd. N.
Regina, SK
S4X 3L2

Pre-Authorized Offerings

Prince of Peace Lutheran Church accepts donations through Pre-Authorized Payment. If you wish to make donations through Pre-Authorized Payment please complete the Pre-Authorized Payment Form and return it to the church or email it to etransfer@princeofpeaceregina.ca.

Interac e-Transfer

Anyone using online banking on a computer or mobile device can send their offerings to Prince of Peace using Interac e-Transfer. Prince of Peace is setup to receive e-transfers automatically. Interac e-Transfers can be sent to etransfer@princeofpeaceregina.ca.
