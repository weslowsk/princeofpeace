Who We Are

We are a congregation of Lutheran Church-Canada. We are located in north west Regina, Saskatchewan, Canada. Our pastor is Pastor Daryl Solie. We have Divine Worship Service and Sunday School at 9:30am every Sunday morning. Holy Communion is celebrated on the first, third, and fifth Sundays of each month. We also have special services on Good Friday at 10:00am, December 24th at 5:00pm and 7:00pm, and December 25th at 10:00am.
