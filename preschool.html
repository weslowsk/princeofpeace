Welcome

Let the little children come to me and do not hinder them for the Kingdom of God belongs to them. (Mark 10:14)

We wish to extend a warm welcome to all children 3, 4, and 5 years of age to enroll in our Christian preschool program.


Staff

Director - Kierstin Castillo


Objectives

  • To provide children opportunities to learn about Jesus Christ as their Friend and Savior and to help them understand that God created them as unique individuals.
  • To provide activities to help children grow spiritually, mentally, socially, emotionally, creatively and physically.
  • To help prepare your child for Kindergarten. (Math, pre-reading, printing, large and fine motor skills).
  • To provide guidance as your child learns to interact with others and participate in group activities.
  • To help and support parents in their God-given responsibility of nurturing their children.
